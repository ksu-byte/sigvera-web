SIGVERA
Born from the Infrastructure

Built by engineers who've seen the audit from the other side.

SIGVERA didn't start in a lab. It started in server rooms, data centres, and control rooms of regulated organizations across the EU. For over a decade, the team behind SIGVERA designed, deployed, and managed critical IT infrastructure for EU institutions, regulated financial entities, and energy sector operators across multiple EU countries. SIEM, firewalls, backup, identity management, monitoring. The full stack.

That experience revealed a fundamental disconnect: organizations invest heavily in security, but struggle to prove they're compliant. Audits still mean weeks of manual evidence gathering, folders full of screenshots, and Word documents that are outdated the moment they're written. Meanwhile, the actual proof sits unused in the systems already deployed.

SIGVERA was built to close that gap. An AI-native platform that connects to the infrastructure an organization already runs and continuously evaluates compliance against regulatory requirements. Automatically, with cryptographic proof attached to every finding.
